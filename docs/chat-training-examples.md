## **Advanced Conversation Patterns**

### **Multi-part Questions**
- "What is Veritus and how much does it cost?"
- "Can you explain your beta programme and how to apply?"
- "What's the difference between your tiers and which is right for me?"

### **Comparison Questions**
- "How does Veritus compare to McKinsey?"
- "What's better - your beta or waiting for full launch?"
- "Veritus vs traditional consulting - what's the difference?"

### **Contextual Follow-ups**
- User: "Tell me about pricing" → AI responds → User: "Tell me more"
- User: "What's the beta?" → AI responds → User: "How do I apply?"
- User: "Veritus features?" → AI responds → User: "Give me an example"

### **Intent-based Responses**
- **Pricing Intent**: Automatically mentions beta discounts
- **Beta Intent**: Guides toward application process
- **Process Intent**: Offers specific examples
- **Comparison Intent**: Highlights unique differentiators

### **Emergency/Urgent Scenarios**
- "I need strategic analysis ASAP"
- "We have a crisis situation"
- "Urgent decision needed by tomorrow"

### **Complex Business Scenarios**
- "We're considering a £50M acquisition in fintech"
- "Board meeting tomorrow, need competitive analysis"
- "Regulatory changes affecting our market strategy"

## **Response Quality Features**

### **Contextual Awareness**
- Remembers previous questions in conversation
- Builds on earlier topics
- Provides relevant follow-up suggestions

### **Intent Recognition**
- Detects pricing, process, timeline, benefits, comparison intents
- Adjusts response style accordingly
- Adds appropriate CTAs

### **Intelligent Routing**
- Complex strategic questions → Veritus referral
- Beta interest → Application guidance
- Pricing questions → Beta discount mention

### **Fallback Handling**
- Graceful degradation for unmatched queries
- Always provides helpful alternatives
- Maintains brand voice and positioning

This comprehensive system ensures AcronIQ Assist can handle virtually any question about the company, product, or processes while maintaining the premium, intelligence-focused brand positioning.
